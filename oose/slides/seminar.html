Team
Structure

How the team is structured

  • Team is headed by Project Manager
  • Project Manager reports to Business Manager or Account Manager
  • Teams consist of:
    • Developers
    • Configuration controller
    • Database administrator

Module leaders &
Defect prevention

Large projects also have Module Leaders. All module leaders report to the project managers and have one or more developers under them.

Defect prevention teams are formed from existing team members and are responsible for defect prevention. Defect prevention attempts to reduce the rate at which defects are introduced into the project.

Software Quality Advisor

  • A Software Quality Advisor interacts with project manager and configuration controller.
  • Software Quality Advisors has independent reporting channel.

Risk
Management

What are risks

Risks are those events or conditions that may occur, and whose occurance, if it may take place, has harmful or negative effect on a project.

What is risk management

Risk management aims to identify and take actions to minimise the effect of risks on projects

Points to be noted

  • Risk management adds additional cost
  • Risks, being probabilistic, are difficult to quantify

Steps in risk assessment

  1. Identify
  2. Analyse
  3. Prioritise

Risk
Identification

Any condition, situation, or an event that can occur and would jeopardise the success of the project constitutes a risk

Risk identification focusses on enumerating possible risks to the project.

  • Checklists of possible risks
  • Surveys
  • Meetings
  • Brainstorming
  • Plan reviews

A project manager can use database to get information about risks and risk management on similar projects.

The manager has to use their judgement and instincts to make decisions regarding risks.

Alternatively, they can consult with the team members and consider their opinion.
