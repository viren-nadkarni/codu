River Crossing Problem: 9 men and 2 boys want to cross a river using a raft which can bear the load of only 1 Man or 2 boys. It is known that, 1 man can row the raft across the river in 7 minutes, 1boy can row the raft across the river in 10 minutes and two boys can row the raft across the river in 5 minutes. Find a sequence of optimum raft movement to ferry all the men and boys to the other side of river using Best-First Search Algorithm.
